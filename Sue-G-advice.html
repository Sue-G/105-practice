Fear of Failure

When you decide to start web development, you're overwhelmed with the fear that you can't do this. This especially happens when coding is completely new to you. At times, you feel like maybe you're not smart enough or good enough.

However, that fear Is the reason you should keep pushing. Web development is a fullfilling career. It keeps you challenged and when you create a website that works and people love, there is a satisfaction that is so amazing. You made the right choice to pursue a career in tech.

Consistency is the Key

When you first start web development you are excited and you build a moment. You are pumped up to make website after website. That is, until you hit your first challenge. You're working on some code but it's not working the way you want. You get so frustrated and you take a break. One week later you haven't touched that piece of code again.

This is a very normal part of progress. The key is to be consistent. Don't lose that momentum. If you have complicated challenges, there are so many people out there who are ready to help. So, reach out to these people. Consistency is your friend.

The more you do it, the better you get. One day you wake up and realize you're the one helping newbies navigate the tech life.
